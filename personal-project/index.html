About Our Community

Located just south of Little Rock, East End offers the perfect balance of rural charm and suburban convenience.

East End is a vibrant census-designated place in Saline County. Whether you are looking for top-rated schools within the Sheridan School District, local family-owned dining, or a quiet place to call home, East End welcomes you. We are known for our tight-knit community, beautiful landscapes, and easy access to the capital city.
